About me

Welcome to my website!
My name is Grace Manthey and I’m a data journalist, amateur photographer and all-around nerd. My passion for data journalism comes from my love of math, data visualization and storytelling.

I was raised in Anchorage, Alaska along with my triplet brother and sister. After high school I spent four years in Hamden, Connecticut where I earned my Bachelor of Arts in journalism from Quinnipiac University, while competing on the acrobatics and tumbling team. Then, I moved to Los Angeles, California to earn my Master of Science in journalism from the University of Southern California. I jumped right into my job as the first data journalist hired at the ABC Owned Television Stations, based at KABC in Los Angeles, and helped build a successful and respected team.

I’m an adventurous person with an enormous travel bug, and I love taking photos of travel destinations, family and friends. My husband and I got married in 2020 (yep, we had a COVID-era wedding!) and outside of traveling, we love to cook, play games and watch all our nerdy TV shows. We currently live in Glendale, California.

Please enjoy the rest of my website below! I have links to my email and resume, as well as links to my work and a small gallery of some photos I have taken.
